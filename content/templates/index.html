Email Automation

Conversations with buyers shouldn’t end after they hit ‘Buy Now.’ With Follow-Up Email Automation, you can set up email campaigns to get fired based on events such when the order was placed, shipped or delivered. Templates are customizable with text placeholders that you can weave into your message. Format with our rich text editor or use your own HTML code.

Product Reviews with Buyer Matching

Keeping track of product reviews is hugely important, yet manually looking out for them can get overwhelming. Our Review Notifications Tool automatically sends out alerts each time you receive a product review. Use our review-to-buyer matching algorithm to quickly respond to your customers, especially if they left a negative review.

VAT Invoice Automation

If you sell in Europe, you know that foreign taxes can be complicated and time-consuming. With our VAT Invoice Automation tool, you spend less time dealing with tax document generation for your customers and more time on your business. Automate the process of creating and sending VAT invoices and never have to worry about it again!

Seller Feedback

Amazon’s measure of why a customer should buy from you specifically, is seller rating. Unfortunately, they do not inform you when you receive Seller Feedback. Our Seller Feedback tool gives you email notifications, dashboard based analytics of your seller performance, as well the ability to send automated mails each time you receive a positive or negative Seller Feedback.
