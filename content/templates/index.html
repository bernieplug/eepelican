Features



Feedback Manager

Reviews and feedback are the most important social proof of your product. Amazon's ranking algorithm places heavy importance on the first 21 reviews received after product launch and beyond. With Feedback Manager, you can set up alerts to track your feedback and always be ready to support buyers by taking quick action. At any time, search all of your feedback and reviews and detect patterns for potential problems.

Ad Manager

Managing PPC Campaigns is complex and time consuming. It's also very easy to quickly burn through advertising dollars if you are not doing it right. Efficient Era's Ad Manager helps you create, modify and manage your Amazon sponsored products, by providing you with new keywords as well as bid and match type recommendations that you can simple accept or reject. Our two-way synchronization with Seller Central data saves you time. NEW!

Email Automator

Even with buyer opt-outs, sending follow-up emails is totally worth it! Our easy to use Email Automator helps you trigger emails when a product is ordered, shipped or delivered. Weave placeholders into your message to dynamically address the buyer by full name, include the product they bought and add links to leave reviews. Create eye-catching emails with our rich text editor or customize the HTML.

Data Warehouse

Overwhelmed with data scattered across Amazon reports, pages and listings? Want the ability to quickly search, sort, filter? Efficient Era's Data Warehouse is a giant repository of YOUR data gathered from multiple sources on Amazon, cleaned, normalized and organized at one single location. Easily export lists so you can slice-and-dice them for advanced analysis, offline retargeting or customer retention.

Returns Tracker

A high return rate can make your product ineligible for FBA and even lead to suspension. With Returns Tracker, you can track return rates on a per product basis and measure how they are contributing to your overall return rate. A buyer is likely to follow up a return request with a negative review. Take action quickly and prevent a negative situation. NEW!

Funnel Manager

If you're not building an email list outside the Amazon eco-system you're missing out on a huge opportunity. An email list allows you to offer discounts and coupons to your audience through Facebook and other social platforms without violating Amazon ToS. But how do you know if your leads are actually converting? Our Funnel Manager matches emails on your list to orders placed on Amazon, and also detects Amazon promotions that were claimed. NEW!

Inventory Analyzer

Want to investigate that mysterious spike in sales? Efficient Era's Inventory Health Analysis Charts help you visually track sales and stock and also provide you with Alerts. Annotate these charts with event tags such as price or name changes, special campaigns or special days like Cyber Monday. These annotations give historical context to sales bubbles and stock outs.

VAT Invoice Automator

If you sell in Europe, you know that VAT invoice generation can be a complicated and time-consuming task. With our VAT Invoice Tool you could either automate the process of creating and sending VAT invoices to every customer who makes a purchase, or chose to send them a link to our VAT invoice generation form that they fill out.
