Review Notifications

Keeping track of reviews is hugely important, yet manually checking your reviews can get overwhelming. Our Review Notifications Tool automatically sends out alerts each time you receive a product or seller review. From there, you can quickly respond to customers, especially if they left a negative review.

VAT Invoice Automation

If you’re selling globally, you know that foreign taxes can be complicated and time-consuming. With our VAT Invoice Automation tool, you can spend less time dealing with tax-related headaches and more time on your business. Automate the process of creating and sending VAT invoices to international customers and never have to worry about it again!

Post-Order Emails

Conversations with buyers shouldn’t end after they hit ‘Buy Now.’ With our Post-Order Email system, you can automatically send emails as soon as a customer makes a purchase. Templates are completely customizable.

Keyword Analytics

With the ever changing Amazon SERP algorithm, it’s important for sellers to know exactly which keywords they are ranking for. Our Keyword Ranking tool allows you to provide a keyword list and then monitor your performance against that list over time. This can be a very powerful input to refining your listing and running PPC campaigns.

Sales Charts

In order to monitor how your products are performing over a period of time, it’s vital to be able to visualize your sales data. Our Sales Charts do that for you, but there’s more! They also allow you to annotate your data with custom events, such as a coupon campaign you launched or a holiday event that you know could have impacted your performance.

Category Ranking

Your product could be listed in multiple categories, and those categories might also evolve over time. Knowing how your products are performing across various categories over time on the same chart can provide useful insights for category placements, a powerful tool if used the right way.

Low Stock Notification

We’ve all been there, and we know how stressful it can be - running out of stock is a problem many sellers deal with. One of the first, yet most important bulwarks against stock-outs is a low stock notification system. Bulk Order Notification

If you’re just relying on Amazon, there’s no way for you to know if you receive a large order. With our Bulk Order Notification tool, you define how large a “bulk order” should be, and our tool will notify you whenever you receive one.
