Power Tools for Amazon Sellers

Your One Stop Shop for: Buyer Reviewer Matching • Amazon Email Automation • Review Notifications • Seller Feedback • VAT Invoice Automation • Bulk Order Notification • Sales Charts

Buyer Reviewer Matching

A buyer leaves you a one-star review. How do you contact them to find out what went wrong and if you can do anything to make any amends? Efficient Era's Buyer Reviewer matching tool accurately links verified reviews to orders and helps you directly contact buyers. Our review notification alerts contain direct links to the review page and to the order details page on Amazon.

Email Automation

With Email Automation, you can set up Follow-Up Email Campaigns that get triggered based on events such when the order was placed, shipped or delivered, including any delays you specify. Weave placeholders into your message to dynamically address the buyer by name, refer to the product they bought and even add a link to the product review page. Format with our rich text editor or modify the HTML code.

Sales Charts with Event Annotations

Seller Central Business Reports provide overwhelming amounts of data that can be hard to process. Efficient Era's Sales Charts help you visually track sales and inventory data for each of your ASINs on a timeline. In addition, you can mark your sales charts with custom events, such as price or name changes, special campaigns or special days like Cyber Monday. These annotations give context to sales spikes and dips.

Seller Feedback Alerts & Automation

Amazon does not inform you if someone left you Seller Feedback. With this tool, you get notified right away. We identify positive and negative seller feedback and let you customize an automated email response for both cases. What's more, you can visually monitor your seller performance through charts and search for feedback by star rating or by marketplace.

VAT Invoice Automation

If you sell in Europe, you know that VAT invoice generation can be a complicated and time-consuming task. With our VAT Invoice Tool you could either automate the process of creating and sending VAT invoices to every customer who makes a purchase, or chose to send them a link to our VAT invoice generation form that they fill out.
