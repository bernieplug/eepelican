Powerful Tools for Amazon Sellers

Alerts • Analytics • Automation

Data Warehouse

Overwhelmed with data scattered across Amazon reports, pages and listings? Want the ability to easily search, sort, filter? Our Data Warehouse is a collection of all of your business data gathered from multiple, disparate sources on Amazon in one single location. Quickly export lists so you can slice-and-dice them for advanced analysis, retargeting or customer retention offline. New!

Email Automation

With Email Automation you can set up Follow-Up Email Campaigns that get triggered based on events such when the order was placed, shipped or delivered, including any delays you specify. Weave placeholders into your message to dynamically address the buyer by name, refer to the product they bought and even add a link to the product review page. Format with our rich text editor or modify the HTML code..

Buy Box Alerts

A competitor overrides your listing while you weren’t paying attention. Happens all the time! It's easy for a fly-by-night seller to steal your Buy Box and replace your beautifully crafted listings with shoddy images and incorrect details. Get notified when you lose the Buy Box, or when your listing was silently modified by malicious bots, competitors, Amazon Warehouse or Amazon Retail.

Review Monitoring

Reviews are, by far, the most important social proof of your product. While positive reviews on your pages are great, you actually want to pay more attention to the negative ones so you can quickly post a public response to manage your reputation. Get email notifications for Reviews and Feedback. Analyze charts to show your performance. Search all of your reviews and feedback within our Data Warehouse.

Inventory Health Analysis

Want to investigate that mysterious spike in sales? Efficient Era's Inventory Health Analysis Charts help you visually track sales and stock and also provide you with Alerts. Annotate these charts with event tags such as price or name changes, special campaigns or special days like Cyber Monday. These annotations give historical context to sales bubbles and stock outs.

Ad Analysis

How do you gauge the profitability of your Amazon Sponsored Products Ad campaigns? How many impressions does it take to make an advertised SKU sell? How is your CTR? What about conversions? Efficient Era's historical Ad Analysis charts provide product level drill down insights, helping you see where your Ad spending is working and where to cut back.

Returns Tracker

When a buyer initiates a return request, chances are, they will follow it up with a negative review. There is a very small window of opportunity to quickly reach out to resolve their problem and potentially prevent a negative situation. With Returns Tracker, you can get daily updates about return requests with exact Order IDs, so you can close the loop effectively. New!

Questions Tracker

Buyers these days pay a lot of attention to questions asked by other shoppers before they go ahead with a purchase. A great way to build your brand is to ensure that customer questions are answered satisfactorily. It just shows that you care! Our Questions Tracker gives you a weekly update of questions asked so you can be part of the discussion. New!

Promotions Tracker

Want to measure the effectiveness of your promotions on Amazon? How many of your one-time-use discount codes are actually being claimed? Amazon does not provide this information but we do! Efficient Era will track your promotion codes and match them to resulting orders. New!

VAT Invoice Automation

If you sell in Europe, you know that VAT invoice generation can be a complicated and time-consuming task. With our VAT Invoice Tool you could either automate the process of creating and sending VAT invoices to every customer who makes a purchase, or chose to send them a link to our VAT invoice generation form that they fill out.
