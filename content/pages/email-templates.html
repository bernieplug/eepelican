Here is a sample email sent automatically each time a purchase is made for a product named PC-Glass. There are 2 main parts to how the email is composed: Theme and the Campaign. Further, there are three specific fields that you need to enter text into:

  • Title : The subject line
  • Email Body : The main body of the email
  • Theme Body : The footer that goes on every email that uses this theme

Building your Email

This will be replaced by the actual text at the point when the email is actually sent out. You can use this template as a starting point and change the text as appropriate. More placeholders can be found on the text formatting toolbar.

A Note About Placeholder Text

The following placeholder text will be replaced by the actual values at the point when the email is being sent.

[Name]: The customer's name.

[OrderId]: The customer's Order ID number, in the format 123-4567890-1234567.

[SellerSKU]: The SKU of the product, as listed on Amazon or Efficient Era.

[ProductTitle]: The title of the product, as listed on Amazon.

[ASIN]: The product's ASIN, in the format B00ABCDEFG.

[QuantityShipped]: The quantity of products shipped within this order in numerical form.

[AmazonMarket]: The marketplace that the order occurred on, in the format com/ca/co.uk/co.jp/de/fr (with no period in front).

[AmazonEmail]: Your Buyer-Seller Messaging email, in the format abc12defg34hi56@marketplace.amazon.com.

$email_body: This is a special placeholder, only available under Email Themes, that displays the text of the Email Body field of a campaign that you apply this theme to.

Important note about $email_body: You can position this field anywhere in the Theme Body and it will get replaced by the text of the Email Body of the campaign that you apply this theme to. If this code is not present, the Campaign's Email Body text will NOT be displayed.
