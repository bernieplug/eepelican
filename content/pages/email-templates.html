Email Automation

Get started with Email Campaigns






With Efficient Era you can create and customize a variety of email types. You can weave text, images, placeholders and insert links for product reviews and seller feedback. Here are some examples to get you started. Feel free to copy and use in your emails.

Thank You Email Template


Dear [Name],

Thanks for purchasing [ProductTitle]​! Your product is on its way!

A quick start guide is included in the box, but more detailed instructions and troubleshooting tips can be found on the product page at:
http://www.example.com/products/[SellerSKU]

If you have any questions at all, hit reply on this email to contact us. We are here to help!

Best Regards,
Thom Bender

Placeholders and Links



Placeholders

Efficient Era provides a variety of placeholders that you can insert into your Email body. These will get replaced by the actual values from your orders dynamically when the email is being sent out.

[Name]: The customer's name.

[OrderId]: The customer's Order ID number, in the format 123-4567890-1234567.

[SellerSKU]: The SKU of the product, as listed on Amazon or Efficient Era.

[ProductTitle]: The title of the product, as listed on Amazon.

[ASIN]: The product's ASIN, in the format B00ABCDEFG.

[QuantityShipped]: The quantity of products shipped within this order in numerical form.

[AmazonMarket]: The marketplace that the order occurred on, in the format com/ca/co.uk/co.jp/de/fr (with no period in front).

[AmazonEmail]: Your Buyer-Seller Messaging email, in the format abc12defg34hi56@marketplace.amazon.com.


$email_body: This is a special placeholder, only available under Email Themes, that displays the text of the Email Body field of a campaign that you apply this theme to.

Important note about $email_body: You can position this field anywhere in the Theme Body and it will get replaced by the text of the Email Body of the campaign that you apply this theme to. If this code is not present, the Campaign's Email Body text will NOT be displayed.

Easy to Insert Links

Efficient Era provides easy to insert links that you can weave anywhere into your email. These are available on the formatting panel of the Email Body:

leave a product review: This will direct them to the page where they can leave a product review.

leave a general review: This will direct them to the page where they can leave a general review.

submit a VAT invoice: This will let them get a VAT invoice.

leave seller feedback: This will direct them to the page where they can leave Seller Feedback.

view order details: This will allow buyers to view the details of their order.


Campaigns and Themes



Each email campaign can be styled with pre-created themes. These are available to change swap out at any time under the Theme drop down selector in the Email Body.

Themes: In general, we recommend you start with one of our pre-created themes:

Plain Text: A simple text based theme with no ability to add images

Post Order Theme: A simple HTML based theme with the ability to add images and links

Post Order Theme 2: A simple HTML based theme with the ability to add images and links

Simple Theme: A simple HTML based theme with no styling with the ability to add images and links

Simple Theme 2: A simple HTML based theme with no styling with the ability to add images and links


Advanced: You can also create more advanced themes with your own HTML styling but we do not recommend it for beginners.
