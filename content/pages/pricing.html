Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Can I track my competition with your tools?

Yes, you can add your competitor’s products by ASIN number to track the positive and negative reviews that they receive as part of product or seller feedback. We recommend that you create a separate(free for tracking upto 10 products) account with us in order to track your competition so that you can manage your dashboard and notifications frequency separately.

Q: What does “Matching Product reviews with Buyers” mean? How does that help me?

Efficient Era believes that contacting reviewers to address negative reviews is a hugely important part of running a successful Amazon business, and so we have developed extremely reliable buyer-reviewer software to help remedy this problem. Other companies have tried to offer solutions to this problem but their methods are often unreliable, either connecting the review to the incorrect buyer or not finding the buyer at all. Efficient Era’s software is much more consistent, allowing you to find the buyer associated with the review every time and reach out to address the problem.

Q: What is VAT Invoice Automation?

Our web tool allows your customers to request VAT invoices directly, without having to contact your customer service. It gathers the needed data for the invoice from your customer and from Amazon, generates the VAT invoice PDF they need, and automatically mails it to the them within seconds. We enable sending a link to this tool as part of an email sent automatically when each order ships.

Q: How do you determine VAT rates for different countries?

The VAT rates for each country are customizable. These can be changed at any time by you.

Q: What aspect of the customization of post-order emails can I control?

As of now, the only customization we provide is in the text of the email templates we provide. You can add templates for each geography in multiple languages. These emails are automatically sent as soon as an order is placed. However, in our next release you will be able to define email triggers that are linked to orders placed, orders shipped and orders received.

Q: What kind of alerts do you provide?

Each time you receive a new review, a negative review, a large order or when your inventory is low, you will receive an automatic notification in your inbox. You can opt out of receiving automatic notifications in favor of a weekly digest.

Q: What if I get more orders than my price plan allows for?

These are not hard limits. Don’t worry, we will inform you if the number of orders processed is beyond the limits set in your pricing plan.

Q: How do you handle account cancellations?

Once you cancel your order we will remove your account from our system. You will need to disable permissions for Efficient Era from Seller Central.
