Frequently Asked Questions


Q: What is the difference between the Scalable Plan and the Flat Pricing Plan?

A: Our scalable pricing model is a usage based model where we charge 0.02 USD when you have an order on Amazon. This is great for small to medium sized sellers, providing you with the lowest possible risk of investment in a tool, because you get charged based on your order volume. We do have a minimum monthly charge of USD 19. For a limited time, we are offering a Fixed Pricing Plan for volume sellers. This plan puts a cap on your monthly charge, so you never end up paying more than 299 USD per month. This plan is great for volume sellers with over 15000 orders a month. Please contact us for details.


Q: What does your charge of USD 0.02/order mean?

A: An order is a single sale on Amazon. Our software processes each order in your seller account and extracts important information about the item sold, the buyer, the sales revenue and more. We also automate emails on your behalf, send you alerts and calculate your profits and returns for each order. This is the reason why we charge on a per order basis.


Q: Do you have a minimum charge for Efficient Era tools?

A: Yes, we charge a minimum of $19 a month.


Q: Why do you have a cap of 10,000 ASINS?

A: Our software incurs infrastructural costs in analyzing your ASINs. We monitor each ASIN separately, regardless of whether it is a parent or child ASIN.


Q: I only need to use one of your tools. Can I pay for just one tool?

A: More and more sellers these days favor tool consolidation over tool spread. Efficient Era is a highly integrated all-in-one tools that helps you optimize your time otherwise spread across disparate systems. That said, you are free to use one or all of our tools, our charge remains the same.
